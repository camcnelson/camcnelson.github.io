Zoetrope Font

This puzzle font was designed to be illegible at first glance, but easily readable once the reader's brain has "adjusted" to the pattern. The name refers to the early mechanism of animation which involved viewing moving frames through a small slit and relying on persistence of vision to fill in missing information. One may find it easier to read by viewing the text slant-wise or by diverting the gaze just slightly.
